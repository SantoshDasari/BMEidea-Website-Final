Our Mission

Our Mission for BMEidea is to empower students of all backgrounds to utilize all that they learned in the class and implement it into real-life projects that improve people's lives!

Our Plan

We plan to allow our members to choose a domain of medical device, narrow down their target to one device, create this device with a group of diverse engineers

Our Vision

We want to see all our members get industry exposure into the world of medical devices and one day become outstanding professionals helping save lives with their creations.
